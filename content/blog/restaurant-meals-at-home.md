We used to spend $200/week on takeout. That's over $10,000 per year!

Then I learned to make our favorite restaurant meals at home. Same taste, fraction of the cost.

Here are the 10 easiest wins.

## 1. Chipotle Burrito Bowls

**Restaurant:** $12 per person = $48 for family  
**Homemade:** $10 total

**The Secret:** Make cilantro-lime rice (rice + lime juice + cilantro)!

**Components:**
- Cilantro-lime rice
- Black beans
- Protein (chicken, steak, carnitas)
- Fajita veggies
- Guacamole
- Salsa

**Time:** 30 minutes

## 2. Olive Garden Chicken Alfredo

**Restaurant:** $18 per plate  
**Homemade:** $12 serves 4

**The Copycat:**
- Cook fettuccine
- Make sauce: butter + heavy cream + Parmesan
- Add grilled chicken

**Secret:** Fresh Parmesan makes it!

## 3. P.F. Chang's Lettuce Wraps

**Restaurant:** $10 appetizer  
**Homemade:** $8 serves 4

**The Mix:**
- Ground chicken
- Water chestnuts
- Green onions
- Hoisin sauce
- Soy sauce
- Serve in butter lettuce

**Time:** 20 minutes

## 4. Panera Broccoli Cheddar Soup

**Restaurant:** $9 per bowl  
**Homemade:** $6 makes 6 servings

**The Method:**
- Sauté onions, add broccoli
- Make cheese sauce
- Combine with broth

**Bonus:** Make big batch, freeze portions!

## 5. Chick-fil-A Nuggets

**Restaurant:** $7 for 12  
**Homemade:** $8 for 30

**The Breading:**
- Pickle juice marinade (the secret!)
- Flour + powdered sugar coating
- Bake at 400°F

**Kids Approved:** They can't tell the difference!

## 6. Starbucks Egg Bites

**Restaurant:** $5 for 2  
**Homemade:** $8 for 12

**Make in Muffin Tin:**
- Eggs + cottage cheese
- Add fillings
- Bake at 300°F

**Meal Prep:** Make Sunday, eat all week!

## 7. Pizza Hut Pan Pizza

**Restaurant:** $15 large  
**Homemade:** $5

**The Pan:**
- Cast iron skillet
- Let dough rise in pan with oil
- Bake at 500°F

**Better:** Control your toppings!

## 8. Panda Express Orange Chicken

**Restaurant:** $10  
**Homemade:** $7 serves 4

**The Sauce:**
- Orange juice + zest
- Soy sauce
- Honey
- Ginger + garlic

**Crispy Coating:** Cornstarch!

## 9. Chipotle Carnitas

**Restaurant:** $12 burrito  
**Homemade:** $15 feeds 8

**Slow Cooker:**
- Pork shoulder
- Orange juice
- Spices
- 8 hours

**Use For:** Burritos, bowls, tacos, nachos!

## 10. Cracker Barrel Hash Brown Casserole

**Restaurant:** $4 side  
**Homemade:** $6 serves 8

**The Mix:**
- Frozen hash browns
- Sour cream
- Cheese
- Butter

**Perfect For:** Brunches, potlucks!

## The Savings

**Takeout:** $200/week  
**Homemade:** $75/week  
**SAVED:** $125/week = $6,500/year!

## Tips for Success

**Start Simple:** Pick one recipe this week

**Batch Cook:** Double recipes, freeze half

**Meal Prep:** Make components ahead

**Special Occasions:** Save real restaurants for celebrations

## Why It's Better

- **Control ingredients** (less sodium, no MSG)
- **Bigger portions** (feed more people)
- **Leftovers** (lunch tomorrow!)
- **Customizable** (make it how YOU like it)

## The Learning Curve

**First Time:** Takes longer, might not be perfect  
**Third Time:** Faster, tastes better  
**Tenth Time:** Quick and delicious!

**Be Patient:** It's a skill!

## My Weekly Rotation

**Monday:** Burrito bowls  
**Wednesday:** Orange chicken  
**Friday:** Homemade pizza

**Savings:** $400/month vs. takeout!
