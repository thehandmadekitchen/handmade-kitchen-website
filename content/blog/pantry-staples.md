Having the right pantry staples makes clean eating so much easier. Here are my must-haves:

## Sweeteners
- Raw honey
- Pure maple syrup
- Coconut sugar

## Oils & Fats
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Coconut oil
- Grass-fed butter

## Grains & Flours
- Organic all-purpose flour
- Whole wheat flour
- Rolled oats
- Brown rice

## Baking Basics
- Baking soda
- Baking powder
- Vanilla extract
- Sea salt

With these basics, you can make countless wholesome recipes!
