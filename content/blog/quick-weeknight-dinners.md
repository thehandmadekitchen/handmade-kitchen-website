5:30pm. You just got home. Kids are starving. You have NO idea what's for dinner.

Sound familiar?

These 20 meals save me on crazy nights. All done in 30 minutes or less!

## The Formula

**Quick Protein + Quick Carb + Quick Veggie = Dinner**

**Examples:**
- Rotisserie chicken + rice + frozen broccoli
- Scrambled eggs + toast + salad
- Canned tuna + pasta + peas

## 20 Quick Dinner Ideas

### 1. Sheet Pan Sausage & Veggies
Slice sausage, toss with vegetables, roast 20 minutes at 425°F

### 2. Quesadillas
Cheese + whatever's in fridge, cook in skillet, serve with salsa

### 3. Pasta with Jarred Sauce
Upgrade: Add Italian sausage, sauté vegetables

### 4. Stir-Fry
Any protein + any vegetables + soy sauce + rice. 15 minutes!

### 5. Breakfast for Dinner
Scrambled eggs, toast, fruit. Kids LOVE this!

### 6. Tacos
Ground beef + taco seasoning + tortillas. 20 minutes tops!

### 7. Grilled Cheese & Soup
Upgrade sandwich with tomato, use canned soup

### 8. Rotisserie Chicken Bowls
Store-bought chicken + rice + veggies. Assemble!

### 9. Flatbread Pizza
Naan + sauce + cheese + toppings. Bake 10 minutes!

### 10. Pasta Carbonara
Pasta + eggs + bacon + Parmesan. 20 minutes, fancy!

### 11. Chili Mac
Box mac + can chili + cheese. 15 minutes!

### 12. Burrito Bowls
Rice + beans + salsa + toppings. No cooking needed!

### 13. Chicken Caesar Wraps
Rotisserie chicken + Caesar dressing + romaine + wraps

### 14. Shrimp Scampi
Shrimp + garlic + butter + pasta. 18 minutes!

### 15. BBQ Chicken Sandwiches
Rotisserie chicken + BBQ sauce + buns + coleslaw

### 16. Fried Rice
Leftover rice + eggs + frozen vegetables + soy sauce

### 17. Tuna Melts
Canned tuna + mayo + bread + cheese. Toast!

### 18. Chicken Tortilla Soup
Rotisserie chicken + salsa + broth + beans. Simmer!

### 19. Egg Fried Rice
Rice + scrambled eggs + peas + soy sauce

### 20. Pantry Pasta
Pasta + canned tomatoes + whatever vegetables you have

## The Quick Cooking Tricks

**Trick 1:** Start water boiling FIRST  
While water boils, prep everything else

**Trick 2:** Use frozen vegetables  
Pre-washed, pre-cut, cook in 5 minutes!

**Trick 3:** Buy rotisserie chicken  
Use 3-4 meals: bowls, soup, wraps, salad

**Trick 4:** Cook pasta while making sauce  
Everything done at once!

**Trick 5:** Sheet pan meals  
One pan, 20 minutes, minimal cleanup

## Shortcuts That Work

**Use:**
- Pre-cut vegetables
- Rotisserie chicken
- Frozen vegetables
- Canned beans
- Jarred sauce (upgrade it!)
