---
title: "No-Bake Peanut Butter Energy Bites"
description: "These easy 5-ingredient energy bites are perfect for lunchboxes and after-school snacks. Naturally sweetened with dates and packed with protein!"
category: "Kid Snacks"
image: "https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1558326567-98ae2405596b"
prep_time: "10 minutes"
cook_time: "0 minutes"
total_time: "10 minutes + chilling"
servings: "20 bites"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup natural peanut butter (or almond butter)"
  - "1/3 cup honey or maple syrup"
  - "1 cup old-fashioned oats"
  - "1/2 cup mini chocolate chips (use dairy-free if needed)"
  - "1/2 cup ground flaxseed"
instructions:
  - "Mix peanut butter and honey together in a medium bowl until smooth and well combined."
  - "Add oats, chocolate chips, and flaxseed. Stir until everything is evenly mixed."
  - "Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes (this makes them easier to roll)."
  - "Roll into 1-inch balls using your hands. If mixture is too sticky, lightly wet your hands."
  - "Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks, or freeze for up to 3 months."
notes: "Make Ahead: These actually taste better after sitting for a day! Roll and store in the fridge. Nut-Free Option: Use sunflower seed butter instead of peanut butter. Add-Ins: Try adding shredded coconut, dried cranberries, or chia seeds for variety."
---
