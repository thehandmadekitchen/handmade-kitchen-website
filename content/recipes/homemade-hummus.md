category: "Appetizers"
image: "https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1571070703017-ca2d7e1a4a3b"
prep_time: "10 minutes"
cook_time: "0 minutes"
total_time: "10 minutes"
servings: "8 servings"
ingredients:
  - "2 cans (15oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed"
  - "1/3 cup tahini"
  - "1/4 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2 lemons)"
  - "2 cloves garlic"
  - "1/4 cup olive oil, plus more for drizzling"
  - "1/2 teaspoon cumin"
  - "1/2 teaspoon salt"
  - "2-3 tablespoons cold water (as needed)"
  - "Paprika for garnish"
instructions:
  - "Add tahini and lemon juice to food processor. Process for 1 minute, scrape sides, process for another minute. This makes it extra creamy!"
  - "Add olive oil, garlic, cumin, and salt. Process for 30 seconds."
  - "Add half the chickpeas and process for 1 minute. Scrape sides."
  - "Add remaining chickpeas and process for 1-2 minutes until very smooth and creamy."
  - "While processing, drizzle in 2-3 tablespoons cold water to reach desired consistency."
  - "Taste and adjust seasonings - add more salt, lemon juice, or garlic if needed."
  - "Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with paprika."
  - "Serve with vegetables, pita bread, or use as a spread!"
notes: "Secret to Ultra-Smooth Hummus: Process tahini and lemon juice first! This creates a creamy base. Some people also peel the chickpeas for extra smoothness (but it's not necessary).
